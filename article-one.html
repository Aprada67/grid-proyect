1. practice meditation every day

It doesn't matter 5 minutes or half an hour, time is insignificant, the important thing is to acquire this new habit in your life. My advice is to find a time in the day that you know you will have free and you will be at home.
It is not worth dealing with the stopwatch and going against the clock. If necessary, set the alarm a little earlier and then you will have no excuse for not meditating.
A good idea is to prepare a special corner for this moment, for this offering to yourself. You know, a place in the house where harmony flows freely. Place a rug, get a meditation zafu. Give it imagination and feel that place as if it were sacred!
