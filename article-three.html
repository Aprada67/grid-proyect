3. Includes conscious breathing

In the car, at work, with your family... Anywhere and anytime! Look for the opportunity to stop just to be aware of your breath.

Extend your awareness to the areas involved in the spontaneous act of breathing,such as the chest, nostrils, throat, or abdomen. Just witness the in-breath and out-breath for a few brief moments. Feeling what effects they have, accompanying them with your attention and without trying to modify them.

You will see, how this simple exercise can have an extraordinary impact on your daily well-being, lower your mental revolutions and take you to a state of indescribable calm.
